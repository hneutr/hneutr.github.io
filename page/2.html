Trainsway

Originally Written 2012/01/13

Date: 2015/08/18

Kind: Written

Tags: verse

From side to side, cradled in limbic

Untime, a midpoint in some non-line, an

Ocean half sketched, dawn in silence.

An island around, I am floating

Away, carrying some empty id, a

Path found purpose, listing in between.
