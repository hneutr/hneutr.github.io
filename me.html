Hello! The header doesn't lie - I'm Hunter Wapman.

For me, happiness is a verb, to be found in doing, not in being. I'm happiest when I'm engaged, working toward a goal, solving a problem, improving - myself or my surroundings.

I graduated in 2015 from Tufts University with a degree in Computer Science. I dig complexity theory, machine learning, algorithms, and systems design. There's nothing more satisfying than learning about the way things work.

Brought up on Legos, I have a soft spot for the discrete, and love things I can count or otherwise break down into pieces. I'm a consumate structuralist, and am constantly trying to figure out the best way to store information and things.

I like to spend my time listening to music (space disco, rock), reading (literature, philosophy), exercising (running, swimming, jumprope, rowing), and playing classical piano (particularly Bach).
